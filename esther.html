Esther Wong

About me

Hi there! I'm Esther and I am an undergraduate Computing student at Imperial College London, originally from Hong Kong. My interests include music and art, but I decided to pursue Computing as I am always amazed by the increasing number of technological innovations taking place today. Therefore, I wanted to be a part of this and learn how to utilise technology to create practical programs with real-world uses, in the hopes of one day contributing to the innovation.

My technical skills include Haskell, Java and SQL, having created many small projects during my time at university. Prior to university, I also created and developed an iPhone game using Swift, which helped to advance my knowlege of iOS development.

Why this topic?

My passion in art and computing lead me to gain interest in this topic. I was curious to see how real-life objects could be rendered so realistically. After doing thorough research on graphics and rendering models, I felt like I have gained a good basic understanding of how people render materials, metals in specific. I hope that I will have the opportunity to dive deeper into this field in the near future.
