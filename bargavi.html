About Me

I am a first year Computing student of Imperial College London. Originally from India, I lived and was educated in Dubai. I enjoy travelling to new places and experiencing new cultures, but I also like to relax with a good book. I am proficient in Python, Java, Haskell, and SQL, and have a basic understanding of HTML and XML.

Why this topic?

As an avid gamer, I have always been interested in the technical aspects of what goes on behind the scenes, from the development, to the testing and debugging. This topic offered an insight into the very basics of rendering graphics, and allowed me to learn more about the techniques and challenges in developing something that at a glance seemed so simple.
